Welcome to **MasteringX**, the fastest way for creators to create and sell stunning, professional-quality video based courses without writing a single line of code.

Whether you're a coach, instructor,  or subject-matter expert, **MasteringX** allows you to bring your expertise to life with little setup and maximum attention on what you're teaching.

**MasteringX** aims to deliver a contemporary, fast, and powerful video-delivering platform built on Laravel, VueJS, and TailwindCSS. It is intended to be simple to use, adaptable, and scalable.


### Key Features

* **Clean, Powerful Admin Panel**

  Manage your entire platform from a streamlined dashboard. Courses, lessons, payments, articles, and users all in one place.

* **HLS-Optimized Video Streaming**

    Deliver smooth, buffer-free streaming with **HLS (HTTP Live Streaming)** perfect playback across mobile, desktop, and slow networks. Upload once, stream like a pro.

* **Structured Course Builder**
 
    Create rich courses with modules, lessons, video uploads, and updates scaling your content over time with ease.

* **Markdown Lesson Descriptions**

    Each lesson supports Markdown for clean, readable descriptions, transcriptions, or SEO content. Text and video go hand in hand.

* **Built-in Article System**

    Publish educational blog posts, or SEO articles right from your dashboard and turn readers into paying students.

* **Stripe-Powered Payments**

    Launch monetized courses fast with secure global payments via Stripe. Supports one-time payments or subscriptions.

* **Flexible Access Controls**

    Offer free previews, locked lessons, or full premium access. Tailor visibility based on user access level.
